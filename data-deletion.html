Data Deletion

How to delete your data from Fuel Finder

Fuel Finder does not collect any personal data. There are no user accounts, no server-side data storage, and no personal information to delete.

What Data Is Stored?

Fuel Finder stores a small amount of data locally on your device only:

  • Your preferred fuel type (if "Remember selection" is enabled)
  • Cached fuel station data for offline viewing
  • API authentication tokens (in your device's secure storage)

None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers, because we don't have any servers that store user data.

How to Delete All Local Data

To remove all data stored by Fuel Finder on your device:

On iPhone / iPad:

  1. Go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage
  2. Find and tap Fuel Finder
  3. Tap Delete App
  4. This removes the app and all its locally stored data

On Android:

  1. Go to Settings → Apps → Fuel Finder
  2. Tap Storage & cache
  3. Tap Clear storage to delete all data (or Clear cache for cached data only)
  4. Alternatively, tap Uninstall to remove the app and all data

Common Questions

Q: How accurate are the prices?
Prices come directly from the UK Government's mandatory reporting system. Retailers are legally required to report price changes, so data is typically accurate to within a few hours.

Q: Why can't I see a station near me?
The app shows stations that report to the UK Government Fuel Finder service. Some very small independent stations may not be included.

Q: Does the app work outside the UK?
No ‚Äî Fuel Finder only covers fuel stations in the United Kingdom (England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland).

Q: Does the app track my location?
Your location is used on-device only to show nearby stations. It is never sent to our servers or shared with anyone. See our Privacy Policy for details.

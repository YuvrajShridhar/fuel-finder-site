FAQ

Common questions.

How accurate are the prices?
Prices come from the UK Government's mandatory reporting system. Retailers are legally required to report price changes, so data is typically accurate to within a few hours.
Why can't I see a station near me?
The app shows stations that report to the UK Government. Some very small independent stations may not yet be included in the data.
Does the app work outside the UK?
No ‚Äî Fuel Finder UK covers fuel stations in England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland only.
Does the app track my location?
Your location is used on-device only to show nearby stations. It is never sent to our servers or shared with anyone. See our Privacy Policy.
Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. No subscription, no account required. The app is supported by non-intrusive ads.
